                                                      Flannigon calda 16 days ago

                                                      Hey I've had that 'YOU GOTTA CHANGE QUICK' stress a few times.
                                                      If you're playing without music, just play deliberately slower - my fingers freak out when the muscle memory isn't quite there yet.
                                                      If you're playing along to a song on something like Spotify, try a slower song and come back to the one you're having trouble with.
                                                      The easiest and fastest way I've found I can see improvement is with songs on Youtube. If you can't quite keep up with a song yet, adjust the playback speed to 0.5x or 0.75x and work your way up.
                                                      In short, every time I've been struggling so far, if I play slower, and build up speed, it all pans out!

                                                      Best of luck!
